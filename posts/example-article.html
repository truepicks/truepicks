How to Gain Weight Safely: A 30‑Day Plan

Medically friendly tips you can use today.

This guide is for educational purposes and not a substitute for professional medical advice.

1) Calorie Surplus, But Clean

Target a modest surplus of 250–400 kcal/day with high‑quality proteins, complex carbs, and healthy fats. Avoid sugar spikes.

2) Sample Day

  • Breakfast: Oats + peanut butter + banana + milk
  • Lunch: Rice + chicken + veggies + yogurt
  • Dinner: Roti + daal + eggs + salad
  • Snacks: Nuts, dates, lassi

3) Track & Adjust

Weigh twice a week. If no progress, increase calories by ~150/day. Focus on sleep and hydration.

Pro tip: Strength training 3x/week helps convert calories to lean mass, not just fat.
